My Perfect Day 

    So cute, I tire to adore her
    so beautiful , I lose
    so thoughtful that I let go
    so everything, my wishes come true

    Every night I tell myself 
    I would love you more
    As if by some magic
    you manage to love me more

    How, is a question I care not 
    about and why would I?
    But how you still feel inadequate 
    Is my biggest mystery.

    Everything was translucent
    I froze there, why?
    No clue if I was lost or 
    was I just under and overload.

    I couldn't believe.
    Cleared the blurry screen
    It was my pink plushie
    Smiling so wide.

    Wide enough to enclose
    my smile and all my love
    Wife enough to make me
    feel my family is bigger now.

    Not that it was just us two,
    But eyes just prefer to see
    what they want, and yes
    they had you today.

    They were so hot, your lips,
    Mine just melted onto them.
    So melodious, your voice,
    As if I could hear your smile.

    If I could just, hold on to you,
    Grab you tight, not let you go
    It'd be heaven, I'd wait though
    Mom won't allow it right now.

    A day with my loved ones,
    the two who make my life
    How could it be better...
    With just a bit more of you.
