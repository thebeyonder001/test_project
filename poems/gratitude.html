Gratitude

Long it had been
Since I was standing
It just wasn't my time
And suddenly it was

Such soft pillow
Like I'd ever have
And a warmth
Unknown since long

I effortlessly laid 
Down in your embrace
Nothing hurt anymore
No worries of past

It was such bright
But with you in between
No light could possibly
Disturb my sleep

What great deeds must
Have I done, that just when 
things went bleak, 
YOU opened the curtains

What love must have
I showered on world 
That I could
Shower with you

Yes, there are days
When all we see are clouds
But trust me the rain 
Afterwards is worthy of wait

You are rest to my eyes
Peace to my storm,
Not just love and care,
You respect me

Whether it's sun or
moon, cloudy or
Runny day, health or
Sickness, I would be there

These words are not
Remotely enough, for me 
To express the mature &
Childish love for you

If only I could,
I'd write thank you
In the sky, to tell how 
Grateful I'm for you
