Why I Love You

I have no idea why
Yet i could go on & on
But I'd be counting fruits
And not the seeds

I also have no idea
Why sunsets have become 
so peaceful, why
Flowers seem so beautiful 

No clue whatsoever
how songs became so
vibrant and how did closing 
my eyes became so easy

You light me up,
help me calm down
lighten me bright
That's why i love you

It gives me warmth that
had been missing for so long
and reasons to wake up happy
That's why i love you

It brings upon me a smile
that i can't explain
Just like how i can't explain
Why i love you
