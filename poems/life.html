Life

Roses grow with thorns,
crops with weeds, our 
muscles with stress and 
love with challenges.

Don't ask for simple life
or pray for straight roads;
life moves better with adventures 
Just like rivers flow with curves

Stop hesitating in taking a step 
We still have a marathon left
Fear not of the step but 
Of not taking it

I promise you this, 
As long as I'm in your prayers
or I see you in the moon
In the darkest of the nights

I'll be there to carry you
On the days it's tough
to get up; I'll hold you up
with my own strength.

What's even left to worry?
However hot the sand may be
the brightest of the sun and the 
Clearest of waters awaits us
