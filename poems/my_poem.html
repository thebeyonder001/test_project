My Poem

Peace was never with me
Until I held you,
Sleep was something i never knew
Until you held me.

Why am i even writing?
And what even?
Don't you know already everything
that i can say possibly

You are my little child;
somehow, capable of caring for
everything but yourself, well
ain't I enough for that?.

if you knew, or had an idea
of what all you deserve:
moon, stars and everything
beyond horizon; you'd shush up.

I think of you, dream of you,
wonder of a life with you,
Don't you too? of waking up with me,
sleeping, smiling, laughing with me.

Why not.. right now?
One smile? I'll crack a joke
You can show me a laugh?
And maybe stay like that always?

I wish for the day
when all you know how to do
is smile wide, widest
not as much as our kids maybe

Yes, there's that smile.
Recall of how from nothing at all,
we became something, then more
and now everything to each other.

Let your happiness show
let it trickle out your face
such beauty, such radiance
And that's my poem right there.
