Surprises

she just knows somehow
I guess i'm a simpleton
dropping clues 
everywhere i go
everything i do

she calls me her strength;
I remembermy those times
her words were my bonfire
keeping me warm;
she's more than everything.

her toughness is a sponge now,
for me, soft and
soaking everything up;
the moments we have 
when together.

So much teasing to 
make up for the time apart.
Not much words were said; 
but we understood 
the assignment just well

So, she too drops signs
on her cheeks,so red,
her lips, so plump,
smile, so wide
just like her eyes

this piece of writing lacks
tone and rhythm; she,
though, matches me as if 
god composed our 
notes together

Times change they say. 
But for me and her,
we'd stay the same;
i'd keep bringing her the
same rose and chocolate.
