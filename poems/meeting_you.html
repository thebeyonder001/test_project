Meeting You

Tensed up, anxious, uptight 
That's all i am until
that cool breeze starts and
the flowers start blooming

I approach this source of
spring, beauty, peace
Now i can feel the fragrance too
Of the world you are for me

Resting my head on your bosom
It's so warm, as if I've
Fallen in love with sleeping
I wonder what heaven that'd be

Hugging you so close
and me lost in the curtain
of your hair, pure intoxicant,
Never wanting to come out

Then you sit besides me
Oh, that mellow i feel when
    I cover you like a blanket and
winters become so warm

You move closer, and
go for my cheeks, they 
had been waiting for a
loving embrace for so long

Ah, that softness against me
just for a jiffy, yet eternity long
My worries bid me farewell
All that's left is you and me

All this while, time just flying by
and that moment comes
When we must promise 
to meet again

No regrets though, 
Whether an hour or just a jiffy
Tells me, with you,
how beautiful would an eternity be
