You

The Sun isn't mine
Neither is the moon
But y'know, my day 
rises and sets with you

I have my own blanket
And designated pillow, yet
nothing helps me sleep better
than you and your bosom

It's not like I'm mature or 
even understanding at all. Though,
Somehow when it comes to you
I am the man I have desired to be.

I didn't know I could take lead or
guide through streets & crowd,
arrange dinner or even bargain.
Being 'the man' was a distant dream.

With you, everything is new,
more accurately, better,
somehow so homely. Do I
know you since years?

I wonder today about my 
curiosity; It never settles.
You came out of nowhere
and yet, I didn't bat an eye.

With you, why what and how
never crossed my mind
Is it sorcery or just some
feelings you inculcated.

Be that, as it may,
I feel content. I feel loved.
My questions no more
matter to me; but you.

It isn't that I pray like angels 
Or I am calm all the time
but I find serenity and
peace ever since I'm with you

Neither a session of running
Nor a page of my diary does
What your image does to me
In just a moment of it.
