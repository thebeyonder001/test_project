Sunset

I can name a few colours
But hues.. not really
What shades were they;
Yellow, purple, orange?

Sometimes out the window
Sometimes you beside me
I was either getting filled up 
By sunshine or your love

It was overwhelming;sad too, 
but such an unknown mellow
For Heaven's sake Neither i could 
Nor i wanted to stop

Maybe that's what you saw
When you say i was tearing up
It was just too much to 
take in all together at once

What did i do to deserve all this?
You next to me, embracing my arms
And such scenic beauty out there
Am i just lucky or was it me?

I have no words to tell you 
what was i feeling
Never have I felt such serious
lack of words and colours

You in my arms and
that beautiful sky
I didn't want to let go of any,
which sane person would?

If only that moment could freeze
For atleast an eternity
I'd try to put everything
In words and paint....

Your hair, your fragrance,
You skin, your eyes,
Your arms, your bosom,
Me and that sunset
